   Uncompress the 'Gorillaz AudioKey Link®' file and run it by double clicking the application file.

To uncompress the PC version, use a program such as WinZip.
To uncompress the Mac version, use a program such as Stuffit Expander.


   Once you have uncompressed the 'Gorillaz AudioKey Link®' application, make sure your cd album is in your computer's cd-rom drive and double click the 'Gorillaz AudioKey Link®' application. The 'Gorillaz AudioKey Link®' will automatically look for the CD. Once the application has confirmed the Gorillaz album, it will automatically open a new browser window, granting you access into "Murdoc's Winnebago" web site. Once there, you will be able to explore all that Murdoc's Winnebago has to offer!!

NOTE: for those PC users that are having some problems just playing the audio of the CD, insert the CD and then exit the auto popup multimedia by hitting your ESC key and then closing the window. Then go to your START button, PROGRAMS > ACCESSORIES > ENTERTAINMENT > CD PLAYER.
